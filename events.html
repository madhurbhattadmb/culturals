Past Events

Cult Nite 2k19

The Annual Mega Cultural Event. One night in the name of music, dance and drama - a night of Euphoria. The best performances from Swara, BeatHackers and Drishya are showcased that night. Everyone waits for this night, and no one wants it to end.

Reverb'18

Annual open mic musical night organised by Swara. The only single event where you get to see some chhupe rustoms turn up on stage and set it on fire with their vocals or instrumental skills. A night with no bounds to music, a night to remember forever.

Xpressions'18

In this wonderful event, every student of IIT Bhilai gets a chance to show what dancing skills they have. With no bounds to dance genres, the stage is always on fire on the Xprssions Night

Theatre Nite'18

An annual event exclusive to the Drishya, where each member puts in all their effort into a single play. With the help of mentors, they encapsulate all their work into performance which is not merely an act but a heartfelt connection with its audience. The drama night has all the glamor an actor awaits, totally worth the time.

TPS Events

The Pixel Snappers has been organising photography competitions of which some are year long and some are monthly. Competitors are given a theme and photos are to be clicked based on the theme. They are given a Nikon DSLR to click the photos.

Model United Nations

An extracurricular activity that made the students learn about international relations, diplomacy and the United Nations. This was meant to engage the participating teams to develop deeper understanding into the current world issues.
