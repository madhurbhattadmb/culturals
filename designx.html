DesignX

 Ever wondered how those magnificent digital artworks are made? The most appealing posters and the elegant logos. This is DesignX, IIT Bhilai. Formed in 2019, DesignX was managed by Renaissance, and now works as an independent club. DesignX maintains the brand of Culturals, IIT Bhilai and is the design backbone of all the fests in IIT Bhilai. Members of DesignX are highly skilled in Photoshop, Illustrator and After Effects. DesignX team ups with different clubs and societies take charge of the decorations and digital designs of all the festivals and cultural programmes of IIT Bhilai.


DesignX looks forward to organise workshops on designing for the people who are eager to learn designing. They want to bridge the gap between fine arts and digital arts and dream to make new artworks which involve the artistic excellence of painters and intellectual design sense of digital artists.
