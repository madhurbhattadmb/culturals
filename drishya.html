Drishya : The Drama Club

 Got that acting ka keeda in you, this might just be your opportunity! With their varied activities and all the enthusiasm, they believe in bringing theatre to another level. Their vision is to get all those interested, a platform to hone each one's individual drama skill. Do join them for all the fun that awaits!
All the world’s a stage, And all the men and women merely players; They have their exits and their entrances, And one man in his time plays many parts. ~William Shakespeare

Theatre Nite : Annual Drama Event

 An annual event exclusive to the Drishya, where each member puts in all their effort into a single play. With the help of mentors, they encapsulate all their work into performance which is not merely an act but a heartfelt connection with its audience. The drama night has all the glamor an actor awaits, totally worth the time.
