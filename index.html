Welcome to the Culturals

Indian Institute of Technology Bhilai

As an emerging IIT, the Cultural Council has the honour of shepherding the burgeoning Cultural scene at IIT Bhilai. Composed of driven and passionate individuals, each a leader of their respective club, the Council is responsible for the myriad of events and workshops, including the annual cultural fest Meraz. At the cultural council it is our goal to ensure that the cultural atmosphere is one which promotes growth, learning and excellence in all pursuits.

Meet the Clubs

The roots of the sublime magnum opus

  • Swara

    The Music Club, IIT Bhilai

    Swara gives you a platform to show everyone the musician within you. Intra club jamming sessions and yearly events shape you up and then you are ready to rule the stage in college fests and Inter IIT Cultural Meet.

  • Beathackers

    The Dance Club, IIT Bhilai

    These are some crazy students who love dancing from their soul. They nurture their skills, work hard and dance with their heart out. From individual skills show to performing as a group, Beathackers is the best place to ignite your passion for dance.

  • Drishya

    The Drama Club, IIT Bhilai

    Got that acting ka keeda in you, this might just be your opportunity! With their varied activities and all the enthusiasm, they believe in bringing theatre to another level. Their vision is to get all those interested, a platform to hone each one's individual drama skill. Do join them for all the fun that awaits!

  • Quizzotica

    The Quiz Club, IIT Bhilai

    The newly introduced club mainly focuses on developing the culture of competitive quizzing in the institute. There are regular practice sessions, and formation of squads which represent the institute at all kinds of regional and national level quizzing competitions.

  • The Pixel Snappers

    The Photography Club, IIT Bhilai

    Pixies, the members of the club, seek to display their love for photography through the consecutive criticism of peers, leisure activities that involve photography, and the promotion of photography in and around the IIT Bhilai campus.

  • DesignX

    The Digital Arts Club, IIT Bhilai

    DesignX is the newly introduced club and is the backbone of every fest and events in IIT Bhilai. Join them to make killer designs in Photoshop, Illustrator, etc.

  • Renaissance

    The Arts Club, IIT Bhilai

    Experience new levels of creativity and see your idea turn into some work of art here. Take part in drawing, painting, charcoal painting and various other fine arts and make the world a more creative place.

Meet the Societies

Creativity with originality

  • GOALS

    General Oratory And Literary Society

    GOALS brings together literature and the art of oration, combing knowledge with flair. It endeavours to arm its members with the communication skills necessary in today's environment and broaden their horizons.

  • FPS

    Film Production Society

    Film Production Society, always buzzing, always on a project. From simple edits to flagship, professional productions with big teams.FPS brings together writers, cinematographers, editors and directors to bring to life pieces of art, at 24 frames a second.
